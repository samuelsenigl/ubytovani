When is check-in and check-out?

Standard check-in is after 14:00, check-out until 10:00. Other times by individual agreement.

Are pets allowed?

We do not accommodate pets, thank you for understanding.

What about night quiet time and parties?

Night quiet time is required from 21:00 to 8:00. The accommodation is not suitable for parties or bachelor(ette) events.

What about parking in the center?

You can drive to the square, check in, and then re-park at one of the parking lots a few minutes' walk from the square (some are paid, some are free). In the summer season, you may sometimes need to show your accommodation voucher when entering the square.

Do you provide breakfast?

A kitchen is available for preparing your own breakfast. Or on the square we can recommend, for example, Bistro Café Friends (2 min walk) or Café Telč (3 min walk).

Activities and Trips

We will help you arrange a program and recommend activities in the area. We will gladly check current availability and experiences upon request.

Right in Telč and surroundings

  • Telč Castle, mysterious underground, St. James Tower and galleries
  • Swimming and nature: Javořice, Roštejn Castle, Čeřínek hill
  • Within reach: Historical town Slavonice, Jihlava (ZOO), Dačice
  • Sport: Horseback riding, tennis, golf and cycling
