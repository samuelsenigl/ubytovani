Tips for trips

Discover the beauties of Telč and the surrounding nature.

  • Tours of Telč Castle
  • Tours of Telč underground
  • St. James Tower
  • Trips to nature: Javořice, Roštejn Castle, Čeřínek hill
  • Within reach: Historical town Slavonice, Jihlava (ZOO), Dačice
  • Sport: Horseback riding, tennis and cycling
  • Aquapark in Dačice
  • Golf course near Telč (Šiška's Mill)
